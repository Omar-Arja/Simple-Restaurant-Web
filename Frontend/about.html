OUR MISSION

TO INSPIRE AND TEACH FAMILIES TO COOK
AND EAT REAL FOOD TOGETHER

I LOVE FOOD!

In this space, we always share fresh, flavorful, (mostly) healthy recipes that I love to make and eat in my real, everyday life. If I wouldn't eat it in real life, I won't put it on the blog. My goal is to inspire you with food that is both approachable AND exciting, whether you're cooking for yourself, your family, your roommates, or your friends. I want you to be so excited about these recipes that you eagerly await 5 pm when you can go home from work and start cooking.
On a related note, I absolutely LOVE seeing the food that you're making. It will make my day if you tag us in your Instagram photos and stories! We love to shout out our favorites on Fridays with our Reader Awards on Instagram Stories.
